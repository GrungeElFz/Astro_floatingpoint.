# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 1, 2025**

floatingpoint. ("us", "we", or "our") operates the [floatingpoint.club](https://floatingpoint.club) website (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding data.

### 1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from visitors. Our website is for informational purposes only and does not include user accounts, comment forms, or analytics trackers.

### 2. Embedded Content

Our website uses Meta's oEmbed feature to display public content from Instagram. When you view pages on our site with this embedded content, your browser interacts directly with Instagram's servers. This interaction is governed by Instagram's own Privacy Policy. We do not store or process any information related to your Instagram account.

### 3. Cookies

Our site does not use tracking cookies. Please see our Cookies Policy for more details.

### 4. Your Rights and Content Removal

Our website uses Meta's oEmbed feature to display public content from Instagram. As we do not hold any user data, there is no personal data to delete from our systems.

If your public Instagram post is displayed on our site and you would like it to be removed, please follow these steps:

1.  Find the URL of your Instagram post that is featured on our site.
2.  Send an email to **contact@floatingpoint.club** with the subject "Content Removal Request."
3.  Please include the link to your Instagram post in the body of the email.

Upon receiving your request, we will promptly remove the embedded content from our website.
